Tips

  • Cursor (↖): navigate and select only — use when you don’t want to draw or edit.
  • To remove areas: use Remove BG (header), the Eraser (🧹), Fill (🪣) with a color, or Fill transparent (▢) to make same-color regions transparent.
  • Magic restore (🪄): if Remove BG or the eraser removed something you wanted to keep, select this tool and paint over that area to bring it back.
  • Use Select to draw a selection, then Copy selection as layer or Crop image to selection.
  • Select the Move tool (✥) to drag any layer — including selection layers — to reposition it.
  • Click Layers to expand the list. The first layer is Original; others are numbered.
  • Use Upload as layer to add another image on top of your canvas.
  • Add text with the Text tool: click to place a box, resize or move it, then click Apply text.
  • Use Undo and Forward in the header to step back or redo changes.
  • No login required — use the editor in your browser, free.
